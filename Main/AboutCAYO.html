NOTIFY ME

FOUNDER OF CAYO

Growing up, I struggled with feeling confident in my own body. I didn’t
have the forms that society often celebrates. It left me feeling
disconnected from the idea of femininity, beauty, and self-love.

Later in life, I made the personal decision to do something about it—
something that wasn’t about changing who I was, but about embracing the
version of myself that made me feel whole.
That experience taught me something powerful: confidence isn’t just about
how we look on the outside, but how we feel within. And sometimes, that
feeling starts with the smallest details—like what we wear underneath it all.

The moment I put on a truly beautiful, well-crafted lingerie set, I
understood what had been missing. It wasn’t just fabric. It was
empowerment. It was self-expression. It was me

That’s why I created CAYO—a brand that isn’t just about lingerie,
underwear, or loungewear, but about the feeling it gives you. I want every
person, no matter their shape or background, to experience that quiet
confidence, that unshakable beauty, that
outfit under an outfit that changes how you carry yourself.

CAYO is more than a brand. It’s self-expression, confidence, and the power
of feeling sexy, loved, and seen—starting from within.

Welcome to CAYO.
